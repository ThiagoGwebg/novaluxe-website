About Us

Excellence, Care, and Trust
in Every Home.

NovaLuxe was founded with a simple mission: to provide more than exceptional cleaning services. We strive to bring peace of mind, trust, and thoughtful care to every home we serve.

We are a family-owned, locally operated business founded by a couple of immigrants who chose to build their future in Northern California. We believe every home has its own rhythm, personality, and unique needs. That's why every service we provide is tailored with attention to detail and genuine care.

Our philosophy is simple: as a service company, our job is to solve problems — not create them. Every visit should leave your home more organized, more comfortable, and give you more time to focus on what matters most.

We believe in growing with purpose. For us, quality will always come before quantity. The success of NovaLuxe is not measured by the number of homes we clean, but by the trust we earn in every home we enter.

Being welcomed into someone's home is a privilege. That's why we approach every visit with respect, discretion, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.
