Safe Cleaning Starts with the Right Products

By NovaLuxe Team• 5 min read• June 2026

Many cleaning products contain harsh chemicals that may leave residues on surfaces, floors, and furniture. While effective on paper, these products are not always the best choice for homes where families, children, and beloved pets live and breathe every day.

Eco-friendly cleaning focuses on using products responsibly while maintaining high cleaning standards. The goal is to reduce unnecessary chemical exposure without compromising results. In a luxury home, there should be no trade-off between impeccable cleanliness and a truly safe environment.

The Professional Standard

Professional cleaners understand proper dilution ratios, application methods, and which products are most appropriate for each surface. Using excessive amounts of cleaning chemicals does not improve performance — in fact, it can lead to residue buildup, damage certain materials, and significantly affect indoor air quality.

"A properly trained cleaning team protects your family, your pets, and the environment — while delivering exceptional, lasting results."

At NovaLuxe, every product in our toolkit carries EPA Safer Choice certification. We apply them in precise amounts, on the correct surfaces, in the proper sequence. This isn't just good for the planet — it's good for your home and everyone in it.

What Eco-Friendly Means in Practice

  • Plant-derived surfactants that clean powerfully without toxic residue.
  • Biodegradable formulas that are safe for pet paws and little hands.
  • No harsh VOCs that degrade indoor air quality after cleaning.
  • Packaging-conscious choices to minimize plastic and environmental waste.

Learn more about safer cleaning products through the EPA Safer Choice Program.

For information about maintaining healthy indoor environments, visit the EPA Indoor Air Quality Guide.
